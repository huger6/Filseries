About Filseries

What is Filseries?

Filseries is a personal entertainment tracking application that helps you keep a record of all the movies and TV shows you've watched or plan to watch. Never forget what you've seen or lose track of recommendations again!

Features

  • Track movies and TV shows you've watched
  • Build your personal watchlist for future viewing
  • Search millions of titles from TMDB database
  • Discover trending and popular content
  • Explore top-rated movies and series

Powered by TMDB

This product uses the TMDB API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDB. All movie and TV show data is provided by The Movie Database.

Open Source

Filseries is developed by huger6. The project is built with Flask, Python, and modern web technologies.
