Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

Filseries is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your username and password (stored securely using industry-standard hashing).
  • Usage Data: We store the movies and TV shows you mark as watched or add to your watchlist.
  • Session Data: We use cookies to maintain your login session.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To manage your account and preferences
  • To store your watched/watchlist history
  • To improve the user experience

Data Security

Your data is stored locally on our servers. Passwords are hashed using secure algorithms. We do not share your personal information with third parties.

Third-Party Services

We use The Movie Database (TMDB) API to fetch movie and TV show information. Your search queries are sent to TMDB to retrieve results. Please refer to TMDB's Privacy Policy for more information.

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us. Upon request, we will permanently delete all your information from our systems.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
